Our Story

Not a Chain.
Just Good Food.

We're not a chain. We're not trying to be. The Corner Market is the kind of place where the owner knows your order, the dough is made fresh every morning, and the pizza names make you laugh before you even take a bite.

Tucked into Concord Street in Holliston, MA, we've been feeding this town with specialty pies, overstuffed subs, and seriously good food since day one. Yeah, Phantom Gourmet found us. But our regulars found us first.

Come hungry. Leave happy. Order the Pickle Pizza — you'll thank us.
